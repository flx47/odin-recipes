Lasagna

Description

Lasagne originated in Italy during the Middle Ages and have traditionally been ascribed to the city of Naples.

The first recorded recipe was set down in the early 14th-century Liber de Coquina (The Book of Cookery).

Source

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
